Our Rivers

Main Fork of the Salmon River

Known as the “River of No Return,” the Main Fork of the Salmon River offers breathtaking scenery, rugged wilderness canyons, and thrilling whitewater. Perfect for both first-time rafters and seasoned explorers.

Desolation & Gray Canyon

Flowing through 80 miles of remote desert, Desolation and Gray Canyon deliver a peaceful yet adventurous experience. Over 60 rapids make it ideal for families and beginners looking for fun, scenery, and a taste of the wild.

Grand Canyon — Colorado River

The most iconic rafting destination in the world. Towering canyon walls, legendary rapids, and unbelievable geological formations combine to create an unforgettable adventure of a lifetime.
