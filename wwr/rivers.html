Our Rivers

Main Fork of the Salmon River

The Main Fork of the Salmon River, known as the “River of No Return,” features deep canyons, rich forests, and thrilling yet approachable rapids. It blends adventure with peaceful drifting through untouched Idaho wilderness—perfect for both new and experienced rafters.

Desolation & Gray Canyon

Flowing through 80 miles of remote desert landscape, Desolation & Gray Canyon offer a quieter rafting experience. With over 60 Class II–III rapids, it’s an excellent option for families, beginners, or anyone who wants a scenic river adventure without extreme difficulty.

Grand Canyon – Colorado River

Towering canyon walls, world-famous rapids, and unforgettable landscapes make the Grand Canyon the ultimate rafting destination. This bucket-list expedition is perfect for thrill-seekers and anyone looking for the most iconic white-water experience in the world.
