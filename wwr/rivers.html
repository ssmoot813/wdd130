Our Rivers

Main Fork of the Salmon River

Known as the “River of No Return,” the Main Salmon offers a perfect mix of thrilling rapids, peaceful stretches, and untouched wilderness. Surrounded by one of the deepest gorges in the United States, this river trip is ideal for families, beginners, and seasoned rafters alike.

Desolation & Gray Canyon

Stretching through 80 miles of remote Utah desert, Desolation and Gray Canyon provide a serene and scenic experience. With more than 60 Class II–III rapids, this route is excellent for those who want adventure without the intensity of bigger whitewater routes.

Grand Canyon – Colorado River

One of the most iconic rafting destinations on Earth. Experience massive waves, towering canyon walls, hidden waterfalls, and ancient geological formations. This legendary river provides a once-in-a-lifetime rafting adventure for thrill-seekers.
