Our Rivers

Main Fork of the Salmon River

The Main Fork of the Salmon River, known as the “River of No Return,” offers breathtaking scenery, deep wilderness canyons, and thrilling white water. This river is perfect for adventurers of all levels, offering a blend of excitement and peaceful drifting through untouched natural beauty.

Desolation & Gray Canyon

Flowing through 80 miles of remote desert landscape, Desolation and Gray Canyon provide a quieter, more reflective rafting experience. With over 60 Class II–III rapids, it’s an excellent choice for families and first-time rafters looking for adventure without extreme difficulty.

Grand Canyon – Colorado River

A world-famous river unlike any other. Towering canyon walls, legendary rapids, and incredible geological formations combine to create a once-in-a-lifetime rafting trip. This expedition is ideal for those seeking the most iconic white-water experience in North America.
