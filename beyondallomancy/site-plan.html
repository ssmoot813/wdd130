Overview

Purpose

Beyond Allomancy is designed to introduce readers to the Cosmere—Brandon Sanderson’s shared fantasy universe. The site provides accessible explanations of its major worlds, characters, and magic systems, helping both new and returning readers understand how everything connects. This site focuses on clarity, visual support, and ease of navigation so that the complexity of the Cosmere feels manageable rather than overwhelming.

This is a fan-created educational resource and is not affiliated with Dragonsteel Entertainment.

Audience

This site is intended for fans of fantasy literature, especially readers exploring or returning to Brandon Sanderson’s works. It is also helpful for newcomers who want a simple introduction to the Cosmere without spoilers or excessive detail.

Typography

Heading Font: Cinzel Decorative

Cinzel Decorative provides an ancient, fantasy-inspired feel that fits the magical theme of the Cosmere while giving the site a polished, immersive look.

Paragraph Font: Lora

Lora is highly readable and works well for descriptive, lore-heavy text. It balances the more stylized heading font while keeping longer passages easy to follow.

Content

Home page

The Cosmere is a shared universe created by author Brandon Sanderson, serving as the foundation for several of his most popular series, including Mistborn, The Stormlight Archive, Elantris, and Warbreaker. This page introduces visitors to the Cosmere’s core ideas and helps them understand how different books and magic systems are interconnected.

This page will guide readers toward learning more about Allomancy and other major magical systems.

Images for the Home page

Allomancy

Allomancy is the metal-based magic system from the Mistborn series. Allomancers ingest metal flakes and “burn” them to unlock specific powers. Each metal grants unique effects, such as enhancing strength, manipulating emotions, or pushing and pulling on metals.

This page explains how Allomancy works, provides charts showing each metal’s effect, and describes how the system fits into the Cosmere’s broader structure of Investiture.

Images for the Allomancy page

Other Magic Systems

Each planet in the Cosmere features a distinct magic system—AonDor in Elantris, Surgebinding in The Stormlight Archive, Awakening in Warbreaker, and many more. Though different in their mechanics, they all stem from the same foundational principles of Investiture.

This page highlights several major systems, explains their cultural and environmental roots, and shows how they fit within the wider cosmology of the Cosmere.
