Other Magic Systems

Explore the many manifestations of Investiture across the Cosmere.

AonDor (Elantris)

AonDor is powered by glowing symbols called Aons. The magic is shaped by geography and the user's understanding of the symbols, enabling healing, energy projection, and environmental effects.

Awakening (Warbreaker)

Awakening uses Breath and color to animate objects, empower abilities, or create sentient constructs. Commands and Intent determine behavior, and more Breath provides greater strength and versatility.

Surgebinding (Stormlight Archive)

Surgebinders manipulate fundamental forces by using Stormlight. Each Order of Knights Radiant bonds specific Surges, granting abilities such as gravitation, progression, adhesion, and more. Ideals strengthen the bond and unlock deeper power.
