Other Magic Systems

Explore the many manifestations of Investiture across the Cosmere.

AonDor (Elantris)

AonDor is powered by glowing symbols called Aons. The magic is shaped by geography and the user's understanding of the symbols, allowing effects such as healing, energy projection, and environmental manipulation.

Awakening (Warbreaker)

Awakening uses Breath and color to animate objects, enhance abilities, or create sentient constructs. Commands and Intent determine how the Awakened object behaves, and more Breath allows for stronger and more flexible effects.

Surgebinding (Stormlight Archive)

Surgebinders manipulate natural forces by using Stormlight. Each Order of Knights Radiant bonds specific Surges, granting abilities like adhesion, gravitation, progression, and more. Through swearing the Ideals and deepening their bond with a spren, Radiants unlock greater power and responsibility.
