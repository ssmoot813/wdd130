Other Magic Systems

Explore the many manifestations of Investiture across the Cosmere.

AonDor (Elantris)

AonDor is powered by glowing symbols called Aons. The magic is shaped by geography and the user's understanding of the symbols, allowing effects such as healing, energy projection, and environmental manipulation.

Awakening (Warbreaker)

Awakening uses Breath and color to animate objects, enhance abilities, or create sentient constructs. Commands and Intent are crucial to shaping the magic and determining how the awakened object behaves.

Surgebinding (Stormlight Archive)

Surgebinders manipulate fundamental natural forces using Stormlight. Each Order of Knights Radiant bonds specific Surges, enabling abilities like gravity manipulation, healing, adhesion, and more. Through their bond with a spren and the swearing of Ideals, their powers deepen over time.
