Allomancy

The metal-based magic system from the Mistborn series.

How Allomancy Works

Allomancers ingest trace flakes of specific metals and “burn” them internally to unlock magical abilities. Each metal grants a unique effect—such as enhanced strength, emotional manipulation, or metal pushing.

The Metals

Allomancy is built around pairs of metals with opposite effects, such as Steel and Iron or Pewter and Tin. Most Allomancers, called Mistings, can burn only one metal. Rare individuals known as Mistborn can burn all the basic metals, giving them a wide range of abilities and making them incredibly powerful.
