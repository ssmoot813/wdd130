Welcome to Beyond Allomancy

The Cosmere is a shared universe linking many of Brandon Sanderson’s most beloved series, including Mistborn, The Stormlight Archive, Elantris, and Warbreaker. This site helps readers understand how the planets, magic systems, and characters are connected. This is a fan-created site and is not affiliated with Brandon Sanderson or his publishers.
